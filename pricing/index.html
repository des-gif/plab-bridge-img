Questions

How is this different from other PLAB question banks?

Every answer is backed by a NICE or CKS reference, the AI questions are grounded in current UK guidance, and the content is verified by a clinician with NHS experience. We're also the only PLAB prep that connects you to real NHS and social care placements through RMP Recruitment.

What does "3-month access" mean?

PLAB 1 preparation typically runs over about three months, so Core and Career are sold as a full exam-cycle of access. You can also pay monthly on Core if you prefer flexibility.

What is the placement support in the Career plan?

CV and interview preparation, plus referral into suitable NHS and social care roles via RMP Recruitment, before and after you qualify. Placement is supported, not guaranteed, and depends on your eligibility and the roles available.

Is this affiliated with the GMC or NHS?

No. PLAB Bridge IMG is an independent education tool by Webb & Hasham. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the GMC or the NHS.
